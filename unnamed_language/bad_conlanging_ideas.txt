#1
'Before', 'In front of', 'behind', 'because', and stuff like that are prefixes
'Within', 'while', 'Inside', 'of', 'is contained in' are infixes
'on', 'after', 'why', 'when', 'how', 'what', 'who', 'whom' are suffixes.
eg. I cried when I was cutting onions.
You put a suffix after all nouns and verbs in 'I was cutting onions'
